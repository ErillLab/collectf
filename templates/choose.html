Welcome to collecTF

CollecTF (a play on words using the French collectif [collective] and the acronym for transcription factor [TF]) is a database of prokaryotic transcription factor binding sites (TFBS) that aims at supplementing and, with time, superseding the current major TFBS databases (RegulonDB and Prodoric).
